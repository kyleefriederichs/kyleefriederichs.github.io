Projects

Research Assistant Projects

Chromic Duo Collaboration
  • Implemented AR object tracking using Vuforia for a collaborative project with Chromic Duo and Texas A&M's visualization classes.
  • Designed a robust content management system in Unity, allowing seamless swapping of Augmented Memory content and incorporating GPS positioning to guide users between benches, activating content only in proximity to these designated locations and using object recognition to scan the benches.
  • Responsible for curating and integrating diverse AR content from multiple classes into a unified project with the theme of promoting ocean preservation.
Optimizing AR Markers
  • Conducted research on how AR marker tracking is affected by external factors in the surrounding environment, such as weather conditions, as well as determining the optimal combination of materials and scaling to perform well in adverse outdoor environments.
  • Additional research explored how changing certain factors of the AR markers, such as material type, affects the accuracy of image tracking.
  • Used a Locobot WX200 robot to introduce artificial jitter and other precise movements to ensure repeatability.
American Bureau of Shipping (ABS) Safety Project
  • Collaborated on the ABS safety project, contributing expertise in set construction, Arduino sensor setup, and speaker installation, enhancing the overall functionality and safety testing capabilities of the project.
  • Assessed whether AR technologies for maritime surveyors are viable in terms of enhancing work quality and safety.
  • Ran hazard perception tests to evaluate the safety of using AR while performing normal tasks as a surveyor, analyzing data from sensors installed into a mock-up ABS ship room.

Dynamic Subdivision Visualizer
