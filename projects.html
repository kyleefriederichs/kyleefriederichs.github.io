Projects

Chromic Duo Collaboration

  • Implemented AR object tracking using Vuforia for a collaborative project with Chromic Duo and Texas A&M's visualization classes.

  • Designed a robust content management system in Unity, allowing seamless swapping of Augmented Memory content and incorporating GPS positioning to guide users between benches, activating content only in proximity to these designated locations and using object recognition to scan the benches.

  • Responsible for curating and integrating diverse AR content from multiple classes into a unified project with the theme of promoting ocean preservation.

    • Optimizing AR Markers

  • Conducting research on how AR marker tracking is affected by external factors in the surrounding environment, such as weather conditions, as well as determining the optimal combination of materials and scaling to perform well in adverse outdoor environments.

  • Additional research is being done in parallel to the main task to explore how changing certain factores of the AR markers, such as changing the material type, may affect the accuracy of the image tracking.

  • We will be running a battery of tests, and in order to ensure repeatability, we are using a Locobot WX200 robot to introduce artificial jitter and other precise movements.

    • American Bureau of Shipping (ABS) Safety Project

  • Collaborating on the American Bureau of Shipping (ABS) safety project, contributing expertise in set construction, Arduino sensor setup, and speaker installation, enhancing the overall functionality and safety testing capabilities of the project.

  • The main goal of this project is to assess whether or not bringing AR technologies to ships for maritime surveyors is viable in terms of enhancing the quality of work, and if it is safe to do so.

  • Part of the experiment includes running hazard perception tests to evaluate the safety of using AR while performing the normal tasks as a surveyor by analyzing the data collected from the sensors installed into a mock up ABS ship room.
